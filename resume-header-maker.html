A professional resume header is the first thing recruiters see. A well-designed header makes your resume look polished and memorable.

What to Include in Your Resume Header

  • Your full name (prominently displayed)
  • Professional title or target role

  • Phone number (optional)

  • Location (city, state)

Resume Header Best Practices

  • Use professional colors (blue, gray, navy)
  • Make your name the largest element
