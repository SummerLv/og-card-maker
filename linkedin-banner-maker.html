Create LinkedIn Banners That Stand Out

Your LinkedIn banner is prime real estate for personal branding. A professional banner communicates who you are and what you do—before someone even reads your headline.

LinkedIn Banner Size Requirements

  • Recommended size: 1584×396 pixels
  • Aspect ratio: 4:1
  • Safe zone: Keep important content away from the bottom left (profile photo covers it)
  • Mobile: 1128×396 visible on mobile devices
  • File format: JPG, PNG, or GIF (PNG recommended for quality)
  • Max file size: 8MB

LinkedIn Banner Best Practices

  • Professional branding: Use colors that match your personal brand
  • Simple design: Avoid clutter—you have seconds to make an impression
  • Strategic positioning: Important text on the right (left side is often covered)
  • Relevance: Include your industry, skills, or value proposition
  • Contact info: Add your website or email if job seeking

Common LinkedIn Banner Mistakes

  • Too much text — banners should be scannable at a glance
  • Low-resolution images — pixelated banners look unprofessional
  • Ignoring the profile photo zone — don't put important content in the bottom left
  • Generic stock photos — custom designs stand out more
