Frequently Asked Questions

What is an OG image?

An OG (Open Graph) image is the preview image that appears when you share a link on social media platforms like Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Discord. It's specified using the og:image meta tag and typically sized 1200×630 pixels.

What's the ideal OG image size?

The standard OG image size is 1200×630 pixels for Facebook and LinkedIn. For Twitter/X, use 1200×675 pixels. Square format (1080×1080) works great for Instagram and YouTube thumbnails. Our tool generates all three sizes.

Is this OG image generator really free?

Yes! OG Card Maker is 100% free with no watermarks, no signups, and no limits. Everything runs in your browser—we don't even have a server. No data is uploaded anywhere. If you find it useful, you can optionally support us with a coffee.

How do I add an OG image to my website?

Add this meta tag to your HTML <head>: <meta property="og:image" content="https://yoursite.com/og-image.png">. Upload the downloaded image to your hosting provider and use its URL. For Twitter, add: <meta name="twitter:card" content="summary_large_image">.
