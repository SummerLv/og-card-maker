Create YouTube Thumbnails That Get Clicks

A great thumbnail is the difference between a video that gets views and one that gets ignored. YouTube thumbnails should be bright, bold, and communicate your video's value in under a second.

YouTube Thumbnail Size Requirements

  • Standard YouTube: 1280×720 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio)
  • YouTube Shorts / Square: 1080×1080 pixels (1:1 aspect ratio)
  • Maximum file size: 2MB
  • Format: JPG, PNG, or GIF (PNG recommended for quality)

Tips for High-CTR YouTube Thumbnails

  • Use contrasting colors — bright yellows, reds, and blues pop against dark backgrounds
  • Include faces with expressive emotions (surprise, excitement)
  • Keep text under 4-5 words — people can't read while scrolling
  • Add an arrow or circle to draw attention to key elements
  • Be consistent with your branding across all thumbnails
