What is Slack Unfurl?

When you paste a link in Slack, it automatically "unfurls" to show a preview with an image, title, and description. This is called Slack unfurling. A custom OG image makes your links look professional and increases click-through rates in Slack channels.

How Slack Unfurl Works

  • Slack reads the <meta property="og:image"> tag from your URL
  • The OG image appears in the link preview
  • Standard size: 1200×630 pixels
  • Slack caches images for ~24 hours

Tips for Effective Slack Link Previews

  • Keep titles under 70 characters for full visibility
  • Use high contrast — Slack has both light and dark modes
  • Include your brand colors for recognition
  • Clear, simple images work best at small sizes
  • Test with Slack's unfurl debugger if needed
