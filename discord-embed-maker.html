Create Discord Embed Images That Stand Out

When you share a link in Discord, it automatically creates an embed preview with the OG image. A custom embed image makes your links look more professional and increases click-through rates in Discord servers.

How Discord Embeds Work

  • Discord reads the <meta property="og:image"> tag from your URL
  • The OG image appears in the embed preview
  • Standard size: 1200×630 pixels (4:3 aspect ratio)
  • Minimum size: 200×200 pixels (will look blurry)
  • Max file size: 8MB

Tips for Effective Discord Embed Images

  • High contrast: Discord's dark mode makes light colors pop
  • Simple text: Users scan quickly — keep it under 5 words
  • Brand colors: Use colors that match your server or brand
  • Consistent style: Use similar designs across all your embeds
  • Test it: Paste your link in Discord to see how it looks
