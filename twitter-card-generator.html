How to Create Twitter Cards That Get Clicks

Twitter Cards are preview images that appear when someone shares your link on Twitter/X. A good Twitter Card can increase click-through rates by 2-3x compared to links without preview images.

Twitter Card Size Requirements

  • Summary Large Image: 1200×675 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio) — recommended
  • Summary Card: 1200×628 pixels (1.91:1 aspect ratio)
  • Minimum size: 300×157 pixels (will look blurry)
  • Maximum file size: 5MB for images

How to Add a Twitter Card to Your Website

Add these meta tags to your HTML <head>:

  • <meta name="twitter:card" content="summary_large_image">
  • <meta name="twitter:title" content="Your Title Here">
  • <meta name="twitter:description" content="Your description">
  • <meta name="twitter:image" content="https://yoursite.com/twitter-card.png">

Tips for Effective Twitter Cards

  • Keep text minimal — people scan quickly on Twitter
  • Use high contrast colors for readability
  • Include your brand or logo for recognition
